Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture.

In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin.

Pigeon Origami

Facts of pigeon
  • Scientific name: Columbidae.
  • They can speed up to 160 km/h (100 miles per hour).
  • They can recognise all 26 letters of the English language.

Cicada Origami

Facts of cicada
  • Scientific name: Cicadoidea.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  • Female cicadas lay from 200 to 600 eggs in tiny holes made in branches and twigs in trees and shrubs.

Facts of chameleon
  • Scientific name: Chamaeleonidae.
  • A change in colour can occur in as little as 20 seconds.
  • The eyes of the chameleon provide 360 degree.
